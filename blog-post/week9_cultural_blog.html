post question-analysis

How do I get to Franklin Avenue?


Franklin Ave. is very long and has a number of twists and turns that I was not aware of. Instead of asking how to get to Franklin Ave. I should have asked for specific cross streets or addresses.


Which language is easier to learn?


People have very different learning styles, knowing WHY or HOW a language is to learn is much more important than assigning an arbitrary number to its difficulty.


May I help?


Rather than asking to help, ask HOW you can help. It goes a long way.
