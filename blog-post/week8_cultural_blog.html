Discuss a time you had an emotional conflict with someone. What was it about? What happened?


A few years back I had a pretty heated arguement with a roommate about how much noise he would make at certain parts of the day and how I only need him to be relatively quiet for a couple of hours each day.


What was the source of the conflict?


A disagreement over what was an acceptable volume for music.


How did you handle that conflict?


He decided he was being quiet enough regardless of what I said, so I begain to be overly loud during other parts of the day when it was clear he preferred the quiet.


Did your actions make the conflict better or worse?


Worse, which was the whole point.


If you could go back, what would you do differently, if anything?


Probably pick a different roommate because we disagreed about a lot of other things and he was not a very clean person. However, I also agree that I intentionally aggravated the situation because I was not happy with it.


What did you learn from this experience?


More flies with sugar than vinegar. Also to pick roommates who had more similar schedules.
